Showcase for Vienna Airport Innovation playground

Introducing a set of delightful sample WebAR experiences for travellers passing through a large airport mobility centre. My Phoenix Soars

Evoking the childlike delight of imagined flight, a colourful bird emerges, as though from behind the viewer, to fly high above the room. It turns in a full circle and returns to the viewer, passing overhead in a colorful display of all its feathered finery. Intended for deployment in large arrival / departure halls, visitors terrace, or even on the tarmac.

Build complexity: Medium Footprint: QR start • 15–30s Best placed: visitors terrace • arrival area

This experience can be calibrated to a preferred location, with targeted viewing angles to enhance that ‘Wow’ moment for viewers.

Please use Android phone.

A soft-landing

A dynamic flight experience, to be enjoyed in tight spaces (e.g. corridors and hospitality, seating areas). An old-fashioned airplane flies from behind the user, when all at once a stream of adventurous bunnies, harnessed in parachutes and riding bicycles gently drift to a soft-landing.

Build complexity: Low Footprint: QR start • 10–20s Best placed: entrances • corridors

This format is remarkably flexible. Characters can be replaced and objects selected that meet the criteria for bespoke brand experiences.

Please use Android phone.

Human-scale
Experience

Airport Party

Each traveller can have their own arrival or departure party with the characters in the game. A happy moment to experience in a queue at departures, security, embarking, disembarking, customs or at duty-free. A human scale experience for travellers of all ages to delight in the moments of departure, arrival and return.

Build complexity: Low Best placed: arrivals • exits Footprint: QR start • 10–20s

This format is again, hugely flexible and can be adapted with event-based and/or seasonal themes. Characters can be replaced and objects selected that meet the criteria for bespoke brand experiences. Currently featuring a Vienna-born cat, a Steiermark hiker, a pilot puppy and a cyclist bunny who says "Servus."

Please use Android phone.
