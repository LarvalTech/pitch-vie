Austrian Airlines Logo-Triggered AR

An experience triggered by the Austrian Airlines logo located on signs, promo stands, or other branded materials around the airport. Final version of the 3D airplane will look like it belongs to your fleet.

Build complexity: High Scales to many variants Best placed: signage • promo stands

Due to its elegant, minimalist composition and smooth color gradients, the Austrian Airlines logo presents limited high-contrast feature points for standard image-tracking models, making it less suited to conventional AR recognition pipelines. That is why, if you select this experience, we will build a custom computer vision model to track it. All we need from you is 120 - 150 photos of the logo in different settings, lighting conditions, and angles (we can guide you through the process to make it as easy as possible).
